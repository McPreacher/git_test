Saying hello to a world that doesn't say hello back

What can we do when we say hello world and the world doesn't answer back? Just keep saying hello world! In whatever language you are learning, whether it's ruby, python, or C, tell the world hello as enthusiastically as possible. Never give up! Here are some programming languages with which you can learn to say "hello world":

  • Ruby
  • Python
  • Fortran
  • C(C#, C++)
  • Lisp(probably hates the world, though)
  • Perl
